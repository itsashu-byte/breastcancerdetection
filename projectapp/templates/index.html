FAQ

Common Questions

How accurate is the detection model?
Our CNN achieves 96% accuracy, 94% precision, and 95% recall on independent test sets validated across three hospital datasets with diverse patient demographics.
Does it replace radiologist review?
No. MammoAI is a decision-support tool. It augments radiologist judgment by flagging suspicious regions. All final diagnoses remain with licensed clinicians.
Is patient data stored or shared?
Images are processed with AES-256 encryption and are not retained after analysis unless opted in. The platform is fully HIPAA-compliant with complete audit logging.
What file formats are supported?
We support DICOM (.dcm), PNG, JPEG, and TIFF. DICOM is recommended as it preserves full radiological metadata for the most accurate results.
How long does analysis take?
Average inference time is under 3 seconds per image. Batch processing of up to 500 scans is also supported for high-volume screening workflows.
